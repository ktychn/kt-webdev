mystical pb-nana wonder

aka katie's birthday cake!

created: january 9, 2025 (katie's birthday)

flavors and construction: chocolate cake with peanut butter frosting and banana filling

design inspiration: envisioned a mossy/leafy/nature-y cake (inspired by pinterest ofc). honestly some of the best swirly frosting i think i've done, i was so happy with how this turned out. the shades of green are just so nice. jane assisted with the creature placement and added the little sprinkle trail which i love!

other notes:

  • gluten free!
  • on the dense side, maybe didn't mix the batter hard enough?
  • made a bonus smaller cake with the extra batter and frosting. added berries and i think this color combo is also so nice!
