regal classic carrot

aka finally making a carrot cake

created: april 26, 2025

flavors and construction: carrot cake with cream cheese frosting and raisins as garnish. no mix-ins bc i don't fw nuts in my cake unlike some of u guys

design inspiration: treva and i have been obsessed with ornate romantic-looking cakes with a lot of drop lines and ruffles so that was the vision. i did the edge and treva did the drop lines, executed beautifully i think. we started running a bit low on frosting toward the end tho lol but were able to make a kind of star-like design on the top? she was beautiful and a mammoth ofc with just frosting, but we decided to add some raisins for fun and i think it is just so cute

other notes:

  • gluten free!
  • so moist omg would highly recommend the recipe we used
  • we don't have a grater so i peeled the carrots into peel bits and treva went nuts cutting them up with scissors inside our measuring cup. crazy method but it actually worked quite well.
